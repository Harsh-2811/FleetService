Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [3/2/2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pakenham Fleet Management. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information.

2. Information Collection

    We collect several types of information from and about our users:

  • Personal Information: Includes data provided for log In, such as your name, email address, and License Number if applicable
  • Driver ID or Employee ID, Assigned job details (e.g., job location, time, and instructions)
  • Real-time GPS location while using the app to track job progress and fleet location.
  • Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Log-in timestamps, job completion details, and app activity.

    • 3. Location Data

    The app collects your GPS location while you are on duty to track job progress, ensure efficient fleet management. You can disable location tracking in your device settings, but this may impact app functionality.

    4. Use of Information

    We collect and process your information for the following purposes:

  • Enhance and tailor your experience in the App.
  • Operate, manage, and maintain the App’s functionality.
  • Communicate updates and troubleshoot issues.
  • Assigning, monitoring, and tracking truck jobs.
  • Real-time location tracking to improve efficiency and ensure safety.
  • Sending notifications about job assignments or updates.
  • Understanding how users interact with the App to improve its functionality.
  • Ensuring compliance with legal and company policies.
    •

    5. Information Sharing

    We only share your information with:

  • Management: To monitor and assign jobs efficiently.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third parties who help maintain the app, such as hosting or analytics providers.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law, to respond to lawful requests.
  • We will not sell, rent, or disclose your information to third parties for marketing purposes.
    •

    6. Data Security

    We employ CloudGaurd CNAPP measures to protect your data. Though no system is infallible, our commitment to securing your information remains stringent. We also implement strict security measures, such as encryption and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

    7. User Rights and Choices

    As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations).
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection.

    • To exercise these rights, contact us at sales@pakenhamcranetrucks.com.au

    8. Data Retention

    We retain your personal data as long as you are an active user of the App or as required for legal or operational purposes. When data is no longer needed, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

    9.Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    The App may use cookies or similar technologies to collect device information and enhance your user experience.
